# Between Sets

When one band finishes and the next one is setting up, dance floors empty out. People pull out their phones, conversations drift, and the energy that was built up during the performance starts to dissipate.

**Between Sets** transforms that downtime into a playful, shared music experience.

As soon as a band's set ends, Between Sets lights up the venue screen and invites the crowd into a cooperative music game via QR code.

People can actively participate in creating and playing songs for each other, or simply enjoy the spectacle and energy of the room while waiting for the next band to take the stage.

## The Game Loop

1. Enter the name of a person for the song.
2. Enter what that person is doing in the song.
3. The room votes on the genre.
4. The song is generated and played for everyone in the venue.
5. While the song is playing, the next round begins.

## Closing the Loop

When the next band is ready to perform, the game wraps up and a QR code is displayed, allowing attendees to download the playlist of songs created during the session.

## Why It Matters

Between Sets turns an unavoidable transition period into a source of creative energy and shared participation, helping venues keep audiences engaged, connected, and on the dance floor until the next set begins.
