# Between Sets — Hackathon Demo VO
*Dupes just talking. Not a narrator, not a trailer, not a manual — him telling you about the thing we built, with the footage rolling under it. ~2 min, room to breathe up to 3. If a line doesn't sound like something he'd say, he should change it on the spot.*

---

## The script

[Generated track playing, crowd footage]

Okay, so. Every AI music demo you've ever seen is one person typing a prompt alone in their bedroom.

We thought that was boring. So we gave the prompt to an entire crowd at once.

[Wordmark]

This is Between Sets.

[Phones scanning QR, name cloud filling up on the big screen]

Everybody in the room pulls it up on their phone — scan the code, you're in, no app. First move: shout out your friend. Names pop up on the big screen in real time, and if enough people shout the same name... congratulations, the next song is about you. You don't get a say in this.

[Intent screen, real submissions]

Then everybody types what they actually want tonight. And people get *honest*. "I want to lose my mind." "I want to text my ex." Doesn't matter — it's all going in the lyrics.

[Tug-of-war on the big screen, the seam bending back and forth]

Then — the genre battle. Tug-of-war. Techno on one side, disco on the other, and you are literally just smashing your phone screen to drag the line to your side. It gets way more competitive than it has any right to.

[The "COOKING" screen with name + genre + intent]

And here's the part we're proud of. The second the vote ends, Claude takes everything — the winning genre, the trending name, all those confessions people typed — and writes a full original song, with your friend's name in the chorus. Suno turns it into a real track.

[Song reveal → crossfade. SHUT UP and let the track play ~8 seconds]

Sixty seconds ago that song did not exist. Now two hundred people are screaming somebody's name to it.

[Footage of the set continuing, maybe dashboard b-roll]

And it never stops — while one song plays, the next round is already voting and already generating. The set builds itself all night. No dead air, no waiting on a render bar.

[Phone: recap playlist, scrolling lyrics]

Then at the end of the night, everybody's phone gets the receipts. Every song the room made, in order, with all the lyrics. Download it, share it. You just made an album with two hundred strangers.

[Wordmark, last track still playing]

That's Between Sets. Claude writes it, Suno plays it, the crowd runs it.

(beat)

Go make some noise.
